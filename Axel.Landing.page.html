Frequently Asked Questions

What insurance do I need to join Axle? +
You need a minimum of $2M in general liability insurance, valid operating authority, and current DOT certification. Our verification team will review your documentation during signup.
How long does verification take? +
Most carriers are approved within 48 hours. If we need additional documentation, we'll reach out immediately.
Can Axle integrate with our existing TMS? +
Yes. Our RESTful API integrates with major TMS platforms including SAP, Oracle, and custom systems. Our integration team handles the setup during onboarding.
Is my data secure? +
Yes. We use bank-level 256-bit encryption, SOC 2 Type II compliance, regular third-party security audits, and role-based access controls.
